CMPT 350.3

Web Programming

  • Subject: Computer Science
  • Credit units: 3
  • Offered: Term 2 only
  • Weekly hours: 3 Lecture hours and 1.5 Tutorial hours
  • College: Arts and Science
  • Department: Computer Science

Description

Focuses on the concepts, technologies and tools needed for the development of web-centric
applications. Special emphasis will be given to client-server programming, scripting, integration
of existing application and high-level networking issues, e.g., use of SOAP.

Prerequisite(s): CMPT 280

Note: Students with credit for CMPT 353 cannot take this course for credit
